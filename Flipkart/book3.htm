Book Description

Manorama Year Books in English is India's most popular GK fact book. The Yearbook is extremely useful for students preparing for various Competitive and entrance exams. It is also useful for teachers, Researchers, Media persons and laypersons alike. It serves as an Up-to-date database covering varied topics like Science and Medicine, Space, Environment, Literature, History, 1000 Quiz, Sports, Policies and Schemes of government, Census reports, Election results, Religious Census report, Economic indicators, Art forms, National Days, new Appointments, list of Union Cabinet Ministers, GK, Awards, Books, Cinema, Sports, etc.
